Step into Coding

Go on, try your hand at some code. You can tell the dog, mario or mickey to move or do anything you want them to do. Make sure you opened this page in Chrome, hit F12 to open up Developer Tools, and try some of these commands in the console:

  1. dog.moveUp(); (or Right, or Left, or Down)
  2. mickey.toggle("explode"); - do it again to make him reappear
  3. mickey.toggle("bounce", {times: 5}, "slow"); - do it again to make him reappear
  4. Now try chaining them together like so: mario.toggle("explode").toggle("explode");

Now that you're a programmer, we'll move on to greater things. First, we'll go to FVI's website and change the pictures to round. Go to fvi.edu, find a rectangular picture and right click it. Click on inspect element, and change the "border-radius" to 100%.

Now we'll go to Microsoft's website and rearrange their pictures as well. See if you can figure out how! Hint: you will need to change the src of each <img> element.
